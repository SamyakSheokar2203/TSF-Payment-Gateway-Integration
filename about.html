ABOUT US

Donation is a non-political, non-profit organisation committed to the development of rural India. Donation began in a remote village in the Chhattisgarh District. It was registered under The Societies Registration Act of 1860. Donation is now working in over 100 villages.

Mission

"To work for the cause and care of disadvantaged humans and to improve their quality of life."

Our approach is to ‘help people help themselves’ by making the disadvantaged self-reliant and enabling them to lead a life of dignity. This philosophy lays the foundation for our programmes so that they have the buy-in of the entire community and are hence sustainable and effective.
We work with and through grassroot organisations that have passion and purpose, but are strapped for funds.
